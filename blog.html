Our certificate and online programs for 2024

Online certificates offer college-level courses in a specific field or topic — but take less time and cost less than a full degree program. Theyre a great way to explore a new area or gain key skills that can take your career Google Career Certificates create opportunities for non traditional talent to kickstart their career in IT, user experience design, data analytics, or project management, thanks to our hiring consortium member companies. The programs are product agnostic and designed to help people land jobs at any company, across any sector. Become a participating employer to connect with skilled candidates and grow your talent pipeline.

About us

Eduford University is a private Ivy League research university in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Founded in 1636 as Harvard College and named for its first benefactor, Puritan clergyman John Harvard, it is the oldest institution of higher learning in the United States.
